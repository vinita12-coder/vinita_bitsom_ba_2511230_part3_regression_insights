# Final Recommendation

## Executive Summary
Based on the regression analysis, the **Multiple Linear Regression** model is recommended for predicting Monthly Sales. It delivered the highest explanatory power with an **R² value of 0.814**, making it the strongest performer among all models evaluated.

---

## Why This Model Was Selected
Three regression models were built and compared throughout the analysis.

| Model               |    R² | Assessment            |
| ------------------- | ----: | --------------------- |
| Marketing Spend     | 0.167 | Moderate              |
| Footfall            | 0.736 | Strong                |
| Multiple Regression | 0.814 | Best Performing Model |

The Multiple Regression model produced the highest predictive accuracy and reflected the combined influence of several key business variables, making it the most comprehensive choice.

---

## Key Business Drivers
The analysis identified four variables with a statistically significant influence on Monthly Sales:

* **Footfall** – The single strongest predictor of sales performance.
* **Marketing Spend** – Contributes positively to revenue growth.
* **Inventory Availability** – Greater product availability expands sales opportunities.
* **Customer Rating** – Higher customer satisfaction is associated with stronger sales outcomes.

Regional dummy variables did not reach statistical significance once operational factors were accounted for.

---

## Business Recommendations

### 1. Increase Customer Footfall
Channel investment into local promotions, seasonal campaigns, and customer engagement initiatives to drive higher store traffic.

### 2. Maintain High Inventory Availability
Minimise stock shortages by strengthening inventory planning processes and improving replenishment cycles.

### 3. Continue Strategic Marketing Investments
Direct marketing budgets toward campaigns with clear and measurable business impact rather than applying uniform spending increases across all channels.

### 4. Improve Customer Experience
Raise service standards, ensure product availability, and enhance after-sales support to lift customer ratings and encourage repeat purchasing behaviour.

### 5. Focus on Operational Improvements
Given that regional effects were not statistically significant, prioritising operational efficiency is likely to deliver stronger results than pursuing region-specific strategies.

---

## Conclusion
The Multiple Regression model provides the most dependable predictions of Monthly Sales and surfaces valuable insights into the factors shaping business performance. By directing attention toward customer traffic, inventory management, marketing effectiveness, and customer satisfaction, management can make better-informed decisions to drive future sales growth and improve overall business outcomes.
